How It Works

Deploy across your entire
data center in hours

No rack shutdowns. No licensed electrician. No rewiring. Clamp each sensor onto the power feed and configure through the Eneron mobile app — servers stay online throughout.

Clamp onto the Cable

Attach the sensor to any power cable 6–21 mm in diameter. No tools, no panel access, no disconnection. Works on live cables during active equipment operation.

Configure via Mobile App

Download the Eneron app (iOS or Android). Activate, connect to Wi-Fi, and calibrate — all guided step by step within the app in minutes.

Data Streams to the Cloud

The sensor transmits live electrical data continuously — power state, load variations, voltage, current. Stored securely in Eneron's cloud with timestamps for every event.

Monitor from Anywhere

Access live server power dashboards via mobile or web at my.eneron.ai. Get instant alerts when a rack drops power or load spikes unexpectedly — from any browser, 24/7.

Critical Detection

Electrical Load Variation Detection

Real-time current monitoring detects abnormal load spikes, sudden drops, or sustained overloads across server circuits. Get alerted the moment current behavior deviates from baseline — before a thermal event or power failure occurs.

Maintenance

Predictive Hardware Failure

Electrical anomalies — irregular draws, power factor shifts, or unstable voltage — are early indicators of failing PSUs, UPS degradation, or cooling load changes. Catch them before they become outages.

Compliance & PUE

Energy Audits & PUE Reporting

Generate rack-level and facility-level energy consumption reports. Track Power Usage Effectiveness (PUE), prove compliance with sustainability targets, and reduce your data center's operating cost with granular data.
